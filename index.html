About Us

Welcome to Events Studio by Globibo – your go-to solution for effortlessly managing and organizing events of all sizes. At Globibo, we're passionate about simplifying event planning and execution, empowering organizers to create memorable experiences seamlessly.

Our Mission

Our mission is to revolutionize the way events are organized and executed. We believe that everyone should have access to powerful tools that streamline the event planning process, from inception to completion. With our innovative platform, Events Studio, we aim to empower organizers, vendors, and attendees alike, fostering collaboration and enhancing event experiences worldwide.

Why Choose Us?

  • Simplicity: We prioritize user-friendly design and intuitive interfaces, ensuring that our platform is accessible to users of all skill levels.
  • Flexibility: Whether you're planning a small gathering or a large-scale conference, Events Studio offers the flexibility to adapt to your unique needs and requirements.
  • Robust Features: From event creation and ticketing to attendee management and analytics, our comprehensive suite of features covers every aspect of event organization.
  • Community-driven Development: We value feedback from our users and actively incorporate suggestions and ideas into our development roadmap, ensuring that Events Studio evolves to meet the ever-changing needs of the event planning industry.

Technologies We Use

  • Ionic and Angular: Our frontend is built using the Ionic framework with Angular, providing a seamless and responsive user experience.
  • SignalR: We utilize SignalR for real-time communication, enabling instant updates and notifications for event organizers and attendees.
  • Microsoft ASP.NET: Our backend is powered by Microsoft ASP.NET, ensuring robustness, scalability, and security for data management and processing.
  • Chart.js: We leverage Chart.js for data visualization, allowing users to analyze event statistics and trends with interactive charts and graphs.
  • PayPal: Integration with PayPal enables secure and convenient payment processing for event tickets and transactions.
  • Firebase: Firebase provides backend services such as authentication, database, and hosting, enhancing the scalability and reliability of our application.
  • Live chat (Tawk.to): We offer live chat support using Tawk.to, allowing users to get instant assistance and support during their event planning process.
